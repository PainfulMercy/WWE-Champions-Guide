Info

The path that led "The Lunatic Fringe" to WWE took him around the world, where he developed the uncanny ability both to absorb and to distribute punishment in equal measure. This has served him well in WWE, first as a member of The Shield, and now as an unrepentant loose cannon who creates his own brand of chaos wherever he goes.
